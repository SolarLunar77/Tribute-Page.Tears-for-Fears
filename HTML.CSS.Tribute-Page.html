Tears for Fears

The British Rock Band that Combined New Wave with Unbridled, Mesmerizing Emotion.

A brief timeline of their musical journey.

  • 1981: Orzabal and Smith became influenced by New Wave and formed Tears for Fears.
  • 1982 - 1983: Their first album, The Hurting, was released and became an international success.
  • 1984 - 1986: Songs From the Big Chair and workdwide fame.
  • 1987 - 1990: The Seeds of Love was released.
  • 1991 - 1992: Break-up and solo careers.
  • 1993 - 1996: Second line-up: Elemental and Raul and the Kings of Spain.
  • 1999: Mercury Records released remastered editions of Tears for Fears' first three albums.
  • 2005: Reunion and the release of Everybody Loves a Happy Ending.
  • 2010 - Present: Touring, remixes, and covers.
